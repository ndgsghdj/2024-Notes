Fractionating Column

Mixture of crude oil is heated so that it enters the fractionating column as a gaseous mixture → ALL the fractions exist as vapour at the same time and then separate out by selectively condensing

Note: During fractional distillation the liquids DO NOT 'boil' or 'evaporate' one at a time

State the relationship between the position of the fractions collected and their respective boiling points.

Carbon-Intensive Sectors

  • Electricity generation
  • Transport industry
  • Heavy industry
    • Types of businesses that carry a high capital cost, high barriers to entry and low transportability, e.g. petrochemical industry, oil refining, aerospace and aviation.
